How to Use the System
Welcome to TwoFeeds Finance — your all-in-one dashboard for managing publisher costs, invoices, team, and revenue. Everything is organized by Year → Month, so the first thing to do on any page is make sure the right period is selected at the top.
🚀 Start of Each Month
  1. Pick the right Year & Month from the dropdowns at the top.
  2. Go to Publishers Cost → upload the monthly cost CSV (or paste a Google Sheet link).
  3. Any publisher flagged in red isn't in Sources Info yet — add it there first, then re-upload.
  4. Go to Internal Sources → upload invoices. The AI fills in the details automatically.
  5. Review the extracted invoice data in the popup, fix anything needed, then hit Confirm.
  6. As invoices get paid, mark them — the payment progress bar in Publishers Cost updates live.
  7. Check Cost Summary for the full monthly breakdown.
📂 What Each Tab Does
🔗
Sources Info — the master list
Every publisher and vendor lives here. Set their type (DSP / RW / MB), logo, manager, and contact. All other tabs reference this list — if a publisher isn't here, it won't be recognized anywhere else.
💸
Publishers Cost
Upload your monthly cost file here. Shows total payable, payment progress, and lets you mark each publisher as paid. Red rows = publisher not found in Sources Info.
👁️
Publisher View
Search for any publisher to see their full cost history across all months with a trend chart. Great for spotting patterns or verifying past payments.
🏦
Internal Sources — invoices
Upload PDF / image invoices or paste a Google Drive URL. The AI extracts vendor, number, dates, and amount automatically. Mark invoices as paid, attach a Proof of Payment (POP) link, and get alerted when invoices are overdue.
📺
Media Buying
Track CPC rates by DSP and GEO. Upload data via CSV. Used to calculate media spend separate from publisher costs.
💵
TF Cost
Internal cost breakdown for TwoFeeds — separate from publisher costs. Enter and track your own expenses per period.
💰
Cost Summary
Auto-calculated. Shows the full cost breakdown for the selected month by category — publishers, media buying, TF costs, and more. No manual input needed.
🏢
Advertisers Info
Manage advertiser accounts — contacts, status, and notes. Referenced in revenue and profit calculations.
📈
TF Profit & Profit Summary & Rev Summary
Revenue-side views. Enter revenue figures and the system calculates net profit, margins, and trends. Rev Summary gives a high-level overview across periods.
🌐
General Overview
Top-level summary of the business — key numbers, trends, and charts across all periods in one place.
👥
Team Roles — admin only
Add and manage team members — name, email, role, campaigns, status, and photo. Expand any row to see the full profile.
🤖 AI Invoice Extraction
Drop a PDF or image onto the upload area in Internal Sources, or paste a Google Drive link. The AI reads the document and fills in vendor, invoice number, issue date, due date, and amount.
A preview popup always appears first — nothing is saved until you click Confirm. All fields are editable in that popup, so you can fix anything the AI got wrong.
If the AI struggles with a particular vendor's format, fill in the AI Instructions field on that vendor's Sources Info row — e.g. "due date is Net 30 from issue date".
Any fields the AI couldn't confidently extract are flagged with a warning after the scan, so you know exactly what to check.
📋 CSV Upload — Publishers Cost
Required columns: Publisher, Amount
Optional columns: Type, Approval Label, Approval URL, Invoice Label, Invoice URL, POP Label, POP URL, Notes
Column names are case-insensitive. Extra columns are ignored. You can also paste a Google Sheet share URL instead of uploading a file.
💡 Tips
🔔 The bell icon in the top bar shows overdue invoices. Click it to see what's outstanding.
▾ Expand any row in Sources Info, Team, or Internal Sources by clicking the arrow — a detail panel opens with full info and editable fields.
↕ Use Edit Order in Sources Info, Team, or Settings to drag and reorder items in the list.
⊞ Use Columns to show or hide columns in any table — your preferences are saved per-device.
🔍 Every list has a search bar — start typing to filter rows instantly.
🗓️ Changes auto-save when you leave a field — no Save button needed for most inline edits.
This guide is updated as the system grows. Last updated: May 2026.

How access works:
👁 View — the tab is visible in the nav for that user

New users have no tab access by default — admin must grant access. Use the ⚡ Access button to expand per-tab toggles per user.
Changes take effect on the user's next login.
⚙️ System Configuration
Auto-Refresh Interval
How often the dashboard re-fetches data from the sheet (in seconds). Lower = more up-to-date but more requests. Recommended: 60.
Session Timeout
Auto-logout after this many minutes of inactivity (no mouse or keyboard). Set to 0 to never time out. Recommended: 30.
Shared App Password
Fallback password for users who don't have a personal password set. Leave blank to keep the current one.
AI Extraction Instructions
Extra instructions sent to Claude when reading invoices. Use this to handle edge cases specific to your vendors — e.g. "always use USD", "the total is the line labelled Net Amount", "ignore VAT lines".
